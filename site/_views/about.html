About Us

OUR METHODOLOGY IS TO ACTIVELY SEEK STUDIO VISITS AND CULTIVATE CONVERSATION WITH ARTISTS AND DEALERS IN ORDER TO CONNECT OUR CLIENTS WITH TIMELY AND EXCITING ART ACQUISITION CONCEPTS. MS. RAND AND MS. VAHEY HAVE COLLABORATED ON CORPORATE PROJECTS FOR A DECADE AND HAVE EXTENSIVE EXPERIENCE WORKING COLLABORATIVELY WITH INSTALLERS, FRAMERS, DESIGNERS, ARCHITECTS AND CORPORATE COMMITTEES. WE ARE MEMBERS OF ARTTABLE AND NEW ART DEALER'S ALLIANCE (NADA).
Molly Pfohl Rand
Ms. Rand has worked in the art world for over twenty-five years. She is an industry leader, managing corporate and private collections. Her experience includes, specialist roles at Sotheby's Contemporary Division and Director roles at a blue chip Gallery. Ms Rand has a BA in History and Art History from Boston College and an MA in Liberal Studies and Museum Studies from NYU. She lives on the Upper East Side of Manhattan with her husband and two high school aged daughters. Molly started her art career with an internship at the Minneapolis Institute of Art and worked as a museum guard at the Walker Art Center.
Pilar Vahey
Ms. Vahey has worked in the art world for over fifteen years. She spent five years assisting a private art advisor with art collections internationally, ten years managing corporate art acquisitions and major rotation projects, and two years at Matthew Marks Gallery. She holds a BA in History and Art History from Connecticut College and completed her MA coursework from Hunter College, CUNY. She lives in Brooklyn with her husband and two young sons. Pilar's first position was as a Curatorial Intern at The International Center of Photography.HOME
